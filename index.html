Get in Touch

Founded in 2010, the organization has consistently made meaningful contributions to the Sikh community by offering Gurmat classes at 24 Gurdwaras, conducting Shej Path, providing training in Tabla and Harmonium, organizing Dastar Sikhlai workshops, weekly Gurmukhi tests, and Dastar competitions, promoting Gatka (Sikh martial arts), hosting Kavita and Lecture Mukable, and organizing summer and winter camps, while also screening Sikh Dharmik movies to foster cultural and spiritual growth. The organization's commitment to nurturing the next generation of Sikhs remains as strong as ever, and we humbly seek the continued support of the community to help further brighten the future of our children in Sikhism.
